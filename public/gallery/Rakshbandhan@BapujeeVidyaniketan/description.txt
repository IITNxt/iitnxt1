IITNXT celebrated Raksha Bandhan – “Technology for a Better Society” at Bapuji Vidyaniketan, Gangavaram, in the presence of esteemed guests Yerukunaidu (RSS), VVL (State Convener, Arogya Bharathi), and Surya Prakash (Convener, Arogya Bharathi, Visakhapatnam). The program emphasized the deeper meaning of Raksha as collective responsibility, highlighting how students can use technology ethically and responsibly for social welfare and national development. Through interaction and reflection, participants pledged to apply knowledge and innovation for the betterment of society, reaffirming IITNXT’s mission of empowering youth with values, discipline, and purpose.
