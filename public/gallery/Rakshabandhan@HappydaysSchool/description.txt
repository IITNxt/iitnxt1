IITNXT conducted Raksha Bandhan – “Technology for a Better Society” on 11-08-2025 at Happy Days School, in the presence of Shri Sriram (Chairman, Happy Days School), VVL (State Convener, Arogya Bharathi), and Surya Prakash (Convener, Arogya Bharathi, Visakhapatnam). The program highlighted the true essence of Raksha as responsibility and service, encouraging students to use technology ethically, safely, and for societal welfare. Participants took a collective pledge to apply knowledge and innovation for the betterment of society and national development, reflecting IITNXT’s commitment to nurturing value-driven, socially responsible youth.
