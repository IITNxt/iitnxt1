On 10-08-2025, IITNXT conducted a meaningful Raksha Bandhan – “Technology for a Better Society” program at the Government Girls Residential Campus, Pedagantyada, Visakhapatnam, in the presence of distinguished guests including Shri Naveen Reddy (Chairman, PAN Trust), Yerukunaidu (RSS), VVL (Convener, Arogya Bharathi), and Gurappa Gonsei (Bharatiya Mazdoor Sangh). The event blended cultural values with social responsibility, emphasizing the role of technology in protecting society, empowering young minds, and building a safer nation. Students and participants took an oath to use technology ethically, responsibly, and for the welfare of society, reinforcing IITNXT’s commitment to nation-building through knowledge, innovation, and values.
