On World Environment Day 2025, IITNXT collaborated with one of the world’s largest student organizations, ABVP Vizag, to organize a Beach Clean-Up Drive at Sagar Nagar Beach, Visakhapatnam, with active participation from IIT students and ABVP Vizag Vibhag students. The initiative reflected a strong commitment to environmental responsibility, community service, and sustainable living, highlighting the role of youth in protecting natural ecosystems. Through collective action and awareness, the program reinforced IITNXT’s mission of using knowledge, leadership, and social responsibility to build a cleaner, greener, and more sustainable society.
